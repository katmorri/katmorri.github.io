MUS 15: Interactive Music for Video Games

Section Lectures

University of California San Diego, Winter 2020

This course is an overview of music composition techniques for video games and other interactive media. It includes weekly main lectures and weekly section lectures. The section lectures reinforce concepts and terminology from the main lectures, review technology skills essential for composing video game music, and provide opportunities to receive individualized instruction from the teaching assistant and collaborate with other students. As time permits, we will discuss advanced topics in video game music, including game audio middleware, sound synthesis, and music composition using artificial intelligence.
